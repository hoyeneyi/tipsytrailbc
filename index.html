FAQ

Got Questions?

Do I need to be 21+?
Yes — all Tipsy Trail Bar Crawl events are strictly 21+. Please bring a valid government-issued ID.
Can I come solo?
Absolutely! Tons of people come solo and meet amazing people along the way. All are welcome.
What does the $40 ticket include?
Your ticket includes VIP entry with no cover charges at any bar, exclusive drink specials at each stop, party hosts, and photo opportunities throughout the night.
How long is the crawl?
Each crawl is approximately 4 hours, hitting 3–4 top bars in the featured neighborhood.
Are drinks included in the ticket price?
Drinks are not included in the base ticket price, but you'll have access to exclusive drink specials at every stop not available to the general public.
Is there a group discount?
Yes! Text us about group pricing for parties of 8 or more. We also do private group crawls for bachelorettes, birthdays, and corporate events.

Questions? Let's Talk.

Group bookings, private events, bachelorette parties — we do it all.
